Description

Pizza mozzarella! Pizza mozzarella! Rella rella rella rella rella

Here is the list of ingredients for making this dish!

  • Warm watter
  • Salt
  • Dry yeast
  • Sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Flour

Steps to create this delicacy!

  1. Add ingredients in the order suggested by your manufacturer. Set bread-maker for dough setting and start machine. When the unit signals, remove dough.
  2. Pat dough into 12x15-inch jelly roll pan or greased 12-inch round pizza pan. Let stand 10 minutes. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C). Spread pizza sauce over dough. Sprinkle toppings over sauce. Bake 15-20 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
